\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The purpose of this class is to model the invoice class. 

This class will demonstrate the attributes members of an \mbox{\hyperlink{class_transportation___management___system_1_1_invoice}{Invoice}} Class. An invoice is generated after the completion of a delivery and it is calculated based on the total mileage, amount of pallets and rate per hour/km.
