Transportation Management Project

Program Introduction

The TMS application is a prototype for the shipping, and logistics industry. It allows for the users to manage the purchasing, schedulling, monitoring and billing of multiple organizations that close a contract to have their order delivered by a partner carrier, whether it is via train or truck.

The application is written mainly in C#. For more information about this programming language, you can visit:

  • "C#" on Wikipedia

TMS Features:

  • Generate a demand and we will find the best transportation method with the best price!
  • Real Time updates and follow up of your delivery!
  • We will take care of your order and assure its integrity and on-time delivery!
